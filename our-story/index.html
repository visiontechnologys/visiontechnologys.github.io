Our Story

A registered Fleur Organic India began its journey in 2020 with a small team in Hasanpur with a vision to provide a high quality honey to people around the world .Founder Vaibhav created Fleur Organic India with a mission to promote ethical beekeeping and good agricultural practices to give back to nature by regenerating natural habitats and restoring biodiversity.

We truly believe that high quality of our product is direct result of compassion and ethical beekeeping. We have always been passionate about wildlife ,particularly bees and the vital role they play in our ecosystems .

About us

Fleur organic India believes in providing the best nutritious food to people around the world as we truly agree with “Let food be your medicine and let medicine be your food ” this has been the guiding force behind Fleur Organic India .

The Tasty Organic Experience:

We believe in sustainable and ethical production .Everything we bring to you is organic and homegrown –produced and packaged under strict quality standards .We believe that great taste comes from the finest ingredients. When you buy Fleur Honey you buy the very best .

Why Fleur Honey ?

We do not use our bees for commercial pollination. Our bee farm is located in a wild place ,so our honey does not have any pesticides or herbicides or other harmful chemicals .
